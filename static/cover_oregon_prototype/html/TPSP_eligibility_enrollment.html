TPSP terms and conditions

You are eligible for a Tribe to pay your entire insurance premium each month. When you enroll in TPSP, you can only shop for insurance plans with other TPSP-eligible household members who qualify for the same plan benefits. In order to take advantage of this benefit, you must accept all of the advanced premium tax credit. If you don't take the advanced premium tax credit, you can still shop for and enroll in plans, and receive your tax credit at a later time. However, a Tribe won't be able to sponsor your premium. Also, please note that your Tribe has only sponsored your premiums through the end of this calendar year. A Tribe may choose to end your participation in TPSP at the end of the calendar year, and then you would be responsible for all remaining premium payments.
