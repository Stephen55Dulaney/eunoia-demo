Welcome to Cover Oregon

Cover Oregon is an online marketplace where Oregonians can compare and enroll in health coverage that fits their needs and budget. We believe health insurance should be affordable, flexible and accessible to everyone.

I'd like to... find health coverage that's good for me and my budget.

Cover Oregon makes it easy to find and apply for medical and dental coverage. You can compare plans side by side so you can make the choice that's best for you. We give you everything you need in one place, in easy-to-understand language.

You can apply for coverage through Cover Oregon even if you have a pre-existing health condition. And even if you have a job, you may qualify for financial assistance to help you with premium and out-of-pocket costs.
