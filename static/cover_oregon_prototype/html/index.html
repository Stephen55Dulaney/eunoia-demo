Cover Oregon Prototype

Healthcare Exchange Interface Design

Case Study Overview

This prototype demonstrates the user interface design for Oregon's healthcare exchange platform, helping residents find and enroll in affordable health insurance plans.

User-Friendly Enrollment

Streamlined process for individuals and families to find and enroll in health plans.

Plan Comparison Tools

Interactive tools to compare coverage, costs, and benefits across different plans.

Financial Assistance

Integrated calculator to determine eligibility for subsidies and tax credits.

Multi-Language Support

Accessible interface supporting Oregon's diverse population with multiple languages.

Key Design Challenges

  • Complex Information Architecture: Organizing vast amounts of healthcare plan data in an intuitive way
  • Accessibility: Ensuring the platform works for users with diverse abilities and technical skills
  • Multi-language Support: Serving Oregon's diverse population with multiple languages
  • Financial Assistance Integration: Seamlessly incorporating subsidy calculations

Design Solutions

  • Progressive Disclosure: Breaking complex processes into manageable steps
  • Visual Hierarchy: Using clear typography and spacing to guide users
  • Interactive Elements: Real-time plan comparison and cost calculators
  • Responsive Design: Ensuring usability across all devices

Impact

This design approach helped thousands of Oregon residents successfully navigate the complex healthcare enrollment process, making healthcare more accessible to the community.
