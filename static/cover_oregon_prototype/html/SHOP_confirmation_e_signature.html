b) Acknowledges that coverage is not in effect until carrier(s) accept(s) this application, establish(es) an effective date of coverage and issue(s) the group contract(s) to the company.*
c) Acknowledges that eligibility standards (e.g., probationary period, minimum hours, etc.) must be established at the time of initial application, may be changed only at contract renewal and must be adhered to for all employees and eligible dependents.
d) Agrees that—except with regard to a statutory continuation of coverage or unless the change is approved in writing by an authorized representative of Cover Oregon—at no time shall any employee be permitted or required to make contributions for coverage at a rate higher than the employee contribution rate represented herein.
e) Agrees the carrier contract(s) will determine all conditions, including procedures, exclusions and limitations relating to the coverage, and will govern in the event of conflict with any benefits comparison, summary or other description of the coverage.
f) Agrees to deliver—or otherwise make available to enrollees—all Cover Oregon and carrier member documents and other coverage-related materials upon request, and also agrees to comply fully with the requirements of Section 2715 of the Public Health Service Act to disclose summary plan and benefit information to eligible and renewing plan participants and beneficiaries.
g) Agrees to make all coverage options available to all eligible employees and dependents who satisfy eligibility requirements.
h) Acknowledges that benefits may be added or deleted only at the time of initial application, at contract renewal, when required by law or as mutually agreed between the company and Cover Oregon in accordance with carrier contract(s).
i) Acknowledges that Cover Oregon must be notified when there is a change to company information (e.g., name, mailing address, phone number, email address, contact person, ownership status, etc.).
j) Certifies under penalty of perjury that all statements made and information provided in this application are accurate and complete to the best of its knowledge or belief, and understands that Cover Oregon will rely in part on the information in this application as the basis for Cover Oregon’s decision to approve this application. I hereby submit this application to Cover Oregon, on behalf of the group, for the group policies indicated within the application. I understand that no coverage is in effect until Cover Oregon and the carrier(s) accept(s) the application and premium deposit and establish(es) an effective date. I understand that premiums are prepaid and are due no later than the 20th of the month prior to the coverage month.

I hereby certify that all enrolling employees meet the eligibility requirements stated within the application. I understand that if I feel Cover Oregon has made a mistake in determining my eligibility for group coverage, I have the right to appeal by contacting Cover Oregon at 1-855-CoverOR (1-855-268-3767). In addition, I hereby appoint the listed agent as our Agent of Record to represent us in matters of insurance benefits provided through Cover Oregon. This appointment is in effect on the same day as this policy, and will remain in force until rescinded through Cover Oregon.
