Finalize

Now for the final step. Please review the user agreement below and use your e-signature to sign the agreement on this site.

User agreement

I certify that I am authorized to complete and submit this application on behalf of myself and all enrolling individuals, and that I:

a) Am applying for the coverage(s) selected within the application

b) Understand that benefits may be added or deleted only at the time of initial application, when I renew my contract, when required by law or as mutually agreed upon between myself and Cover Oregon and the Oregon Health Authority (OHA) in agreement with carrier contract(s)

c) Understand that Cover Oregon/OHA must be notified when there is a change to my personal information or my enrolling dependents’ personal information (such as name, mailing address, phone number, email address, etc.)

d) Agree to provide the Social Security number (SSN) for myself and all enrolling dependents who have SSNs and who apply for the Oregon Health Plan (OHP)/Healthy Kids or premium tax credits under federal law (42 CFR 435.910)

e) Agree to report information about any changes in my eligibility or enrolling dependents’ eligibility to Cover Oregon/OHA within 30 days of the changes

f) Agree to cooperate with the Division of Child Support (DCS) if I receive OHP coverage by helping DCS establish, modify or enforce child support for the child(ren) in my care, and establish paternity (if necessary)

g) Certify under penalty of perjury that all statements I have made and information I have provided in this application are true and complete to the best of my knowledge or belief, and understand that Cover Oregon/OHA will rely in part on the information in this application as the basis for its decision to approve this application.

I hereby submit this application to Cover Oregon/OHA, on behalf of myself and all enrolling individuals, for the policies stated within the application. I understand that no coverage is in effect until Cover Oregon/OHA accepts the application.

I hereby certify all enrolling individuals meet the eligibility requirements stated within the application. In addition, I hereby appoint the listed agent (if applicable) as our Agent of Record to represent us in matters of insurance benefits provided through Cover Oregon/OHA. This appointment is in effect on the same day as this policy, and will remain in effect until canceled through the online portal.

For qualified health plan enrollees I certify that I:

a) Understand that qualified health plan coverage is not in effect until I pay the first month’s premium, and until the carrier(s) accept(s) this application, establish(es) an effective date of coverage and issue(s) contract(s) to me and all enrolling dependents

b) Understand that carrier contract(s) will determine all conditions, including procedures, exclusions and limitations relating to the coverage, and will manage in the event of conflict with any benefits comparison, summary or other description of the coverage

c) Agree to make available all Cover Oregon/Oregon Health Authority (OHA) and carrier member documents and other coverage-related materials to my enrolling dependents upon request

If I have provided untrue, incorrect or incomplete information, I understand that Cover Oregon has the right to adjust the rates originally quoted to the appropriate level retroactive to the date this false information was provided, and I will be required to pay the new rate within 30 days of the date of notice by Cover Oregon.

I understand that no coverage is in effect until Cover Oregon/OHA accepts the application and premium deposit, and establishes an effective date.

For Oregon Health Plan/Healthy Kids enrollees I certify that I:

a) Assign or give OHA or its designee the right to pursue and get reimbursed for medical assistance paid on my behalf from other health insurance, legal settlements or other third parties, including anybody who may be liable to me for an injury he or she caused me or other members of my family receiving medical assistance

b) Agree to notify OHA or its designee and my coordinated care organization when I am pursuing a claim against anybody who injured me or a member of my family who is receiving medical assistance, and, when requested, to provide information that is needed to get the reimbursements

c) Assign or give OHA or its designee the right—if a person who received medical assistance dies—to recover from the person’s “estate” (as defined in ORS 416.350) the amount of medical assistance received by the person starting at age 55

a. This includes monthly payments made by OHA or its designee to coordinated care organizations. In cases where the person receiving benefits is in an institution (such as a nursing home) for six months prior to death, the state will recover money for all medical assistance provided regardless of the person’s age when received.

b. OHA or its designee will not claim this money if the person receiving benefits is survived by a natural or adopted child who is under age 21, blind or meets Social Security Administration criteria as permanently and totally disabled. If the person receiving benefits is survived by a spouse, OHA or its designee will wait until the spouse dies and then submit a claim to the spouse’s estate.

d) Assign or give OHA or its designee the right—if a person who received both medical assistance and Medicare dies—to recover from the person’s estate the amount that OHA or its designee paid on the person’s behalf to the federal government for Medicare Part D prescription drug coverage

a. Effective January 1, 2014, the Oregon Legislature has mandated that reimbursement of Medicare Part D payments made on your behalf will be recovered the same way as medical assistance.

e) Give OHA the right to pursue and get medical support from a spouse or a parent

a. If any child on this application has a parent living outside of the home, I know I will be asked to cooperate with the agency that collects medical support from an absent parent.

b. If I think this parent might hurt me or the child if OHA tries to find information about paternity or health coverage, I can tell OHA and I may not have to cooperate.
