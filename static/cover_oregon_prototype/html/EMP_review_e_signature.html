E-signature

You're almost finished! Just one more step, and your Cover Oregon application will be complete. Please take a few moments to read the user agreement below. Once you've read the agreement and confirmed you have done so by checking the box, you will need to e-sign your application.

User agreement

I certify that I am authorized to complete and submit this application on behalf of myself and all enrolling dependents (if dependent coverage is offered), and that I:

a) Am applying for the coverage(s) selected within the application

b) Understand that coverage for myself and enrolled dependents is contingent on me making any required contributions to my employer through payroll deductions

c) Understand that carrier contract(s) will determine all conditions, including procedures, exclusions and limitations relating to the coverage, and will govern in the event of conflict with any benefits comparison, summary or other description of the coverage

d) Agree to make all of my employer’s documents, carrier member documents and other coverage-related materials available to my enrolling dependents

e) Understand that my employer must be notified when there is a change to my personal information or my enrolling dependents’ personal information (such as name, mailing address, phone number, email address, etc.)

f) Certify that all statements I have made and information I have provided in this application are true and complete to the best of my knowledge

I hereby submit this application on behalf of myself and all enrolling dependents for the coverage indicated.
