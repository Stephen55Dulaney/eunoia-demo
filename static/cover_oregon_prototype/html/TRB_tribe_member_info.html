Why don't I see the name of my Tribe?

Additional benefits for enrolled members of federally recognized Tribes are only available through Cover Oregon. State-recognized Tribes and Tribes outside of the U.S. don't qualify. If you’re certain your Tribe is a federally recognized Tribe, please check the spelling and try again.
