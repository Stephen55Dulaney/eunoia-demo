Shopping groups are determined based on several different factors:

  • your application information and eligibility criteria
  • tax filing status
  • household relationships
  • Federal regulations
  • State policies

Each situation is different and there is not a "correct" selection. Please consider your unique situation and what is right for you when making your selections.

Don’t see a group that works for you?
Contact Cover Oregon Customer Service, an agentor a community partner at no cost to you

The shopping options are intended to help you find the right coverage for you and the members of your household. There is no "correct" selection.

Everyone on the same plan
Choose this option if you want everyone in the group to be on the same medical plan. This may make it faster to shop, easier to manage your coverage and could be the most affordable option. You will receive a single monthly invoice from the chosen carrier.

A different plan for each person
Choose this option if you want to customize coverage for each person in the group. Please note you will have to manage different plans, benefits and health care networks. You will receive a monthly invoice from each chosen carrier. Your overall costs may increase depending on the plans you choose.

Different plans for adults and children
Some families will choose this option to set a different level of coverage for their children. Your overall costs may change depending on the plans you choose.
