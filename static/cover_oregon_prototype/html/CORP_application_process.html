The application process

Our step-by-step process is designed to make it easy to apply for health insurance, with side-by-side comparisons, a range of quality plans and financial help. It's all in one place and in easy-to-understand language.

Here's a quick overview of the application steps.

Individuals and families

Set up an account. If you're new to Cover Oregon, you'll need to set up an account and get your username and password. Every time you return, you'll log in to the same account.

Describe your household. We'll ask a few questions about the people who live in your household, including birth dates and other key details.

Apply for financial help. If you'd like to apply for financial help, you'll need to enter some basic income information. In most cases, we will be able to tell you if you qualify right away.

Shop for a plan. You can compare plans and get all the details you need to find the best medical and dental plan for you. If you'd like, you can even choose a different plan for each member of your household.

Enroll in your selected plan. Review your application, confirm your selections and provide an e-signature. You're all set!

Have this information handy when you apply:

  • Information (e.g., birth dates) about the people in your household
  • Tax-filing information or your tax return (if you have filed before)
  • Pay stubs
  • The name of your agent or community partner, if you work with one

Start Application

Agents and community partners

Cover Oregon's network of certified agents and community partners can help you with your application at no cost to you.

  • Search for an agent or community partner

Employers

Set up an account. If your company is new to Cover Oregon, you'll need to set up an account. You and any other authorized users will all be able to access the same account.

Choose your coverage and set your contribution. You can offer medical coverage only, or add dental coverage too. Choose to offer employee-only coverage or employee and dependent coverage. Then you can set how much you will contribute toward premium costs.

Submit your employee census and get quotes. Cover Oregon gives you two easy ways to submit your census—enter the data online or upload it all at once using our template. Once you're done, quotes from all available insurance companies will automatically be displayed.

Select your plan and set employee choice level. You’ll choose a reference plan and set your contribution. You can offer your employees the reference plan or provide more coverage choices. No matter what level of choice...

Enroll your employees. You'll choose your open enrollment period and can give your employees the option to enroll online through our website.

Have this information handy when you apply:

  • Information about your employees and their dependents
  • Company policies such as your new-hire probationary period and weekly eligibility hours
  • Budget and other plan-selection criteria
  • The name of your agent, if you work with one
