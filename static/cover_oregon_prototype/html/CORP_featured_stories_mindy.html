Featured stories

We want to hear about your experiences with health insurance. Be part of a new chapter in Oregon health.

Mindy Todd, Massage Therapist

Mindy has two full-time jobs: one as a massage therapist at a chiropractic clinic and one as a single mother to 8-year-old Taylor. She loves both her jobs, but neither offer health insurance coverage.
 
Overall, Mindy is healthy at 27 years old, but with a young daughter depending on her, she wants to keep it that way. “It's very important to me to find health care coverage so that I can keep my daughter and I healthy,” Mindy says. She wants comfort in knowing that she doesn’t have to stress about health care costs if an emergency should happen, and that she can take preventative measures with regular check-ups to keep her health on the right track.
 
Mindy tried shopping for private health insurance but couldn’t find a plan that both met her needs and her budget. “It’s very confusing to understand what is all out there. It would be nice if there was a way for me to understand what it is that I'm even looking for,” says Mindy.
 
Mindy looks forward to having Cover Oregon as a resource to help her access affordable health coverage that meets her needs.
