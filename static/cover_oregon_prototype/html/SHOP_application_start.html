Get started

Our step-by-step application is designed to make setting up your company's health coverage plan as simple as possible. At the beginning of each section, we'll give you an overview so you know what's coming next and an estimate of how long the section will take to complete.

Overview

6 Minutes

The entire application process is organized into a few key sections, where you'll be able to:

  • Register your company
  • Choose whether you'll offer medical coverage only, or medical and dental coverage
  • Enter your employee census
  • Choose a reference plan, which will serve as the baseline for your coverage and premium costs
  • Set your employee choice level
  • Set your contributions and open enrollment dates

At any time, you can save your work and come back to complete the process later. If you choose to do this, you will come back through your dashboard, which is your central resource for administering your health plan and makes managing your group coverage easier.

Click "Next" if you're ready to dive in.
