Can't attend in person?

Join the Webex virtual meeting through the link listed in the meeting infromation above. This link will become active 10 minutes before the scheduled start time of the meeting.

WebEx instructions

Cover Oregon is using WebEx technology to give the public the opportunity to watch and listen to meetings from their home or office. Please join 5-10 minutes before the meeting.

Dial the toll-free number below and enter the access code when prompted. You will be asked to enter the unique attendee number given to you when you logged in to the web event – you can either enter the number or bypass it by pressing the # key.

If you have speakers or a headset for your computer, you can skip this entire step and listen to the audio through the Web connection.

All are welcome to join this informative event.

We look forward to seeing you there!
