Enrollment 101

Cover Oregon provides clear information on a range of insurance plans so individuals, families and small business owners can make side-by-side comparisons and choose the right coverage for their needs. We offer health insurance from private insurers and coverage through state programs too.

Who can apply?

Cover Oregon is made for Oregonians, by Oregonians. If you don't have health insurance, can't find affordable health insurance through work, or if you own a small business or buy your own health insurance, you'll be able to access health coverage options through Cover Oregon. Even if you have a pre-existing condition, you can apply for and receive health insurance.

How can I apply?

We encourage all Oregonians to shop for plans and apply for financial help through coveroregon.com, but paper applications are also available. Our website makes it easy to find the right plan. You can also turn to our network of customer service specialists, certified insurance agents and community partners for one-on-one support.

When can I enroll?

For coverage starting in 2014, open enrollment takes place from October 2013 to March 2014. However, some groups can enroll outside of the open enrollment period:

  • Individuals can see if they qualify for health coverage through the Oregon Health Plan and Healthy Kids at any time.
  • Employers can apply at any time. Their employees need to enroll within the open enrollment period set by their employer.
  • Tribes can enroll in the Tribal Premium Sponsorship Program at any time, and Tribal members have special monthly enrollment periods when they can sign up for or change plans.
