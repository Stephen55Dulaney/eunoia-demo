All-Care Health Plan

About Us

AllCare Health Plan is a local Coordinated Care Organization, or “CCO,” providing all of your health care needs (mental, dental and physical). We focus on keeping you healthy and improving your overall health. Your care is provided by a team of primary care providers who coordinate your care when you need to see another doctor or have to go to the hospital. Your care team includes doctors, nurses, dentists, counselors, health educators, care coordinators, and wellness coaches. We have served the Medicaid population in southern Oregon since 1996 and currently provide care to more than 30,000 people.

www.allcarehealthplan.com

Services: AllCare CCO contracts with 1,235 local health care providers to meet your every health care need. Primary care providers, nurse practitioners and pediatricians are responsible for your day-to-day care. You can also receive care from specialty providers such as cardiologists, orthopedic surgeons, oncologists and more. Specialty providers are available with a referral from your primary care provider. Obstetrics care is also available to pregnant women without a referral. You also have access to dental services, rehabilitation services such as physical therapy and speech therapy, mental health counseling, addiction recovery services, and ”Partners in Health” who can help you manage any chronic health condition such as diabetes, asthma or heart problems. You can go to any hospital or emergency department in our service area as well as any skilled nursing facility. With AllCare your prescription medications are covered at local pharmacies. And if you need durable medical equipment like wheelchairs or oxygen, they’re covered, too.
