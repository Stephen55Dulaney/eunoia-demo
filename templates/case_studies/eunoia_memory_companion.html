The Challenge

AI research assistants are increasingly vital for interviews, synthesis, and project management. However, most LLM-based tools lose context between sessions, leading to:

  • Fragmented research continuity
  • Repeated onboarding overhead
  • Lost insights and connections
  • Decreased trust in AI assistance

Business Impact

Teams urgently need:

  • Persistent project memory across sessions
  • Seamless context handoff between agents
  • Auditability of research progression
  • Trust in AI's ability to build on past context
